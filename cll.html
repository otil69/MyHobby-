Carded HotWheels Collection

I started collecting Hot Wheels last year after discovering the hobby while scrolling through social media. That moment marked the beginning of my collection.

Currently, I own a total of 34 carded Hot Wheels and 15 loose (non-carded) Hot Wheels.
During my vacation, I decided to sell a portion of my collection. At the time, I owned over 100 cars, but I chose to sell some to fellow collectors while keeping the ones I truly loved.

In just one year, I have spent more than 10k on this hobby. However, I’ve recently decided to limit my purchases and focus on acquiring only the cars I really want.
